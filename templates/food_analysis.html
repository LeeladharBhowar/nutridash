2. Advanced Analysis Flow

This process uses Google's Gemini multimodal AI to deliver accurate, detailed results.

  • Image Acquisition & Validation:

    The image is secured, validated for format, and prepared for the AI model.

  • Gemini Macro Extraction (Comprehensive):

    AI extracts **Energy, Fats, Carbs, Sugars (Total & Added), Protein, Fiber, Cholesterol, and Sodium** using a structured JSON output.

  • Ingredient Risk Assessment:

    A separate AI call analyzes the **ingredient list** for potentially risky items (e.g., trans fats, excessive additives).

  • Health Score & Recommendation:

    A weighted score (0-100) is calculated based on nutrient quality, and a personalized suggestion is generated.

**Note:** The results will appear instantly on the next screen once the analysis completes.
